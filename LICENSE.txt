This repository contains processed datasets and derived analysis outputs used for
the revised analyses of swim-tunnel respirometry experiments in Japanese eels
(Anguilla japonica).

under the Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0):

  data/raw/

  outputs/tables/

  [Zenodo DOI or repository URL]

When using these data, please cite both the associated journal article and the
archived repository release, once available.

The Python scripts in this repository are not covered by this data license.
They are distributed separately under the terms specified in the LICENSE file.

The processed datasets provided here include individual-level swimming and
metabolic endpoints, speed-step-level MO2 and COT values, statistical summaries,
and figure-summary data. Raw oxygen time-series data are not included in this
repository and may be available from the corresponding author upon reasonable
request.
